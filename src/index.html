Power plant face off

Hover for instructions

In this 1V1 faceoff users take turns feeding and caring for their plants through the use of their buttons. On their turn, users have the ability to choose how many times they will click each button until they think their plant is superior. Then they will submit their results and allow the 2nd player to do the same. Each plant has a specific capacity for its properties: soil, water, light. Over-feed, over-water, or overexpose the plant to light and your plant dies. If one user underfeeds it and the other user has an overall higher total of plant points, that player wins. *treat the game like blackjack with multiple hands*
